Manicaland

Manicaland is a province or state in the eastern part of Zimbabwe. It is also known as the Eastern highlands and it borders Zimbabwe with Mozambique. Besides the Manicaland people's fascinating dialect, Manicaland is mostly known for its mountainous landscape. One of the famous places in Manicaland is the christmas pass shown in the image above. The christmas pass is a 7km stretch of a meandering road within the mountainous terrain that leads into the Mutare city centre, the largest city of Manicaland province
