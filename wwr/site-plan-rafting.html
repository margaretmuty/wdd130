Overview

Purpose

This whitewater rafting one stop website is designed to provide information about our organization and all you will need to know inorder to experience an adventurous, safe, thrilling and unforgatable whitewater rafting experience. This user friendly website unveils tips, gives an insight into whitewater rafting, answer common questions and is the home for all your whitewater rafting researches. As you browse our website, you will find details for rafting beginners, rafting pros, group trips, guided tours, rafting checkist and rules. The website also provide contact details for one on one interaction with a rafting tour guide or consultants, booking information and step by step booking instructions.

Audience

The webiste is created to reach individuals, groups and families or anyone looking for safe, thrilling and fun vacation activities, as well as reseachers, those wanting to train as water rafting guides or anyone who is curious to learn about whitewater rafting. This website addresses questions such as : (1) Who are the service users targeted by this website? This website is designed to target first timers, experienced whitewater rafters, non-swimmers, reseachers and those loooking for information on whitewater rafting or want to train as rafting guides. (2) What type of questions do you expect this website to address? The website provide commonly asked white water rafting safety questions, water rafting distance and rapids, and where and how to find us as well as service costs. (3) How accessible is this site? The website is designed for easy access on any browser over mobile devices, laptops or computer as well as over slow networks. It is easy to nderstand and interact with as it can be accessed in many different languages. The content is clear and comprehensible, and the site is easy to navigate.
