Overview

Purpose

This whitewater rafting one stop website is designed to provide information about our organization and all you will need to know inorder to experience an adventurous, safe, thrilling and unforgatable whitewater rafting experience. This user friendly website unveils tips, gives an insight into whitewater rafting, answer common questions and is the home for all your whitewater rafting researches. As you browse our website, you will find details for rafting beginners, rafting pros, group trips, guided tours, rafting checkist and rules. The website also provide contact details for one on one interaction with a rafting tour guide or consultants, booking information and step by step booking instructions.

Audience

The webiste is created to reach individuals, groups and families or anyone looking for safe, thrilling and fun vacation activities, as well as reseachers and those wanting to train as water rafting guides. It addresses scenarios such as: (1) Who are the target service users? The target service users are first timers, experienced whitewater rafters, non-swimmers, reseachers and those loooking to train as rafting guides. (2) What questions will be answered by them accessing the website? The website provide commonly asked white water rafting safety questions, where and how to find us as well as service costs. (3) How will my audience usually access my site? The website is easy to interact with on a smart phone, laptop or computer.
